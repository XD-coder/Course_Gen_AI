Slide 67: Interactive Exercises - Outlier Removal

Instructions:

Exercise - Detect and remove outliers using Z-score or IQR methods. Consider the following data:

[10, 12, 15, 11, 13, 100, 14, 12, 11, 16, 10, 12, 14, 13, 11, -5, 15, 12, 13, 14]

Quiz

1. Using the IQR method, what are the lower and upper bounds for outlier detection (approximately)?
2. Using the Z-score method (threshold of 3), which values are considered outliers?
3. After removing outliers identified by either IQR or Z-score (with the given parameters), what is the new average (approximately)?

Answer Key:

Question 1: b) Lower: 8, Upper: 18
Question 2: a) 100 and -5
Question 3: b) 12.5
