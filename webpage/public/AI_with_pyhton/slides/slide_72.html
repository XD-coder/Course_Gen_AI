Training and Evaluating a Linear Regression Model

Instructions: Steps to train a linear regression model

  • 1. Import necessary libraries: sklearn.linear_model, sklearn.model_selection, etc.
  • 2. Prepare your data: Load data into NumPy arrays or Pandas DataFrames. Ensure your data is preprocessed (e.g., scaled, cleaned).
  • 3. Split your data: Use train_test_split to divide your data into training and testing sets.
  • 4. Create a linear regression model: Instantiate the LinearRegression class.
  • 5. Train the model: Fit the model to your training data using model.fit(X_train, y_train).
  • 6. Make predictions: Use the trained model to make predictions on the test data: y_pred = model.predict(X_test).
  • 7. Evaluate the model: Calculate metrics like Mean Squared Error (MSE), R-squared, etc. using sklearn.metrics.
