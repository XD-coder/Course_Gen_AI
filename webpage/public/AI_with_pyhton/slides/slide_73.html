Please answer the following questions based on your understanding of simple linear regression in Python.

Question 1:

Which Python library is commonly used for building linear regression models?





Answer: Scikit-learn

Question 2:

In simple linear regression, what does the equation y = mx + c represent?

Answer: y is the dependent variable, x is the independent variable, m is the slope, and c is the y-intercept.

Question 3:

What method is used to fit the linear regression model to the training data in scikit-learn?

Answer: fit()
