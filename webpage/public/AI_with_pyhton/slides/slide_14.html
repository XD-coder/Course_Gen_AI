Slide 14: Control Flow - Quiz

Test your understanding of conditional statements and loops in Python!

Question 1: What is the output of the following code?

x = 5
if x > 10:
  print("x is greater than 10")
elif x > 5:
  print("x is greater than 5")
else:
  print("x is not greater than 5")

Question 2: Which loop is best suited for iterating a known number of times?

Question 3: What is the purpose of the break statement in a loop?

Answers:

Question 1: x is not greater than 5 (c)

Question 2: for loop (b)

Question 3: To exit the loop immediately (b)
