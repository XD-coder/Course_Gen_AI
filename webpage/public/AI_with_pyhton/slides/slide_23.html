Slide 23: Exercise - Variable Assignment

Instructions: Create variables of different data types and print them.

Question 1: Create a variable named `age` and assign it your age as an integer.

Correct Answer: `age = 30` (replace with your age)

Question 2: Create a variable named `name` and assign it your name as a string.

Correct Answer: `name = "Your Name"` (replace with your name)

Question 3: Create a variable named `height` and assign it your height in meters as a float.

Correct Answer: `height = 1.75` (replace with your height)

Question 4: Create a variable named `is_student` and assign it a boolean value indicating whether you are a student or not.

Correct Answer: `is_student = True` (or `False` depending on if you are a student)
