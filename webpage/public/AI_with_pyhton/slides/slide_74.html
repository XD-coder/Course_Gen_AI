Question 1: Which of the following is a key assumption of multiple linear regression?





Answer: A) Linearity between independent and dependent variables.

Explanation: Multiple linear regression assumes a linear relationship between independent and dependent variables. Multicollinearity should be avoided, and residuals should ideally be normally distributed and homoscedastic.

Question 2: Which metric is commonly used to evaluate the performance of a multiple linear regression model?





Answer: C) R-squared

Explanation: R-squared (Coefficient of Determination) is commonly used to evaluate the proportion of variance in the dependent variable that is predictable from the independent variables.
