Interactive Exercises: Using train_test_split

Instructions: Exercise - Train a linear regression model and evaluate its performance.

Question 1: What is the purpose of train_test_split?




Correct Answer: b) To split the data into training and testing sets.

Question 2: What does the test_size parameter in train_test_split control?

Correct Answer: The proportion of the dataset to include in the test split.

Question 3: Why is it important to use a separate test set to evaluate your model?

Correct Answer: To get an unbiased estimate of the model's performance on unseen data. Training on the test data would lead to overfitting and an overly optimistic performance estimate.
